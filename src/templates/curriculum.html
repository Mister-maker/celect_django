Nasscom Curriculum

SSC NASSCOM is the authorised assessment body for IT-ITeS vocational areas for NSQF Level 5 & IT-ITeS Qualification Packs (QPs). There are several entry level roles in the software industry which helps the individuals in performing the key activities by clearing such levels as NSQF Level 4 which is Junior software developer , NSQF Level 5 which provides a job role of Web developer, NSQF Level 7 which provides a job role of Software developer and Junior Data Associate which gives specialized knowledge of Software programming languages and helps in understanding different research process.
